Selection rules:
- "quote" must be an exact substring of {chunk_text}. Do not rewrite it.
- Prefer full clauses or full sentences over heading fragments.
- Prefer lines that connect concepts, claims, methods, results, and limitations.
- If the chunk looks like a table, formula block, figure caption, or code block, use "structural_artifacts" to preserve that clue.
- Any array may be empty if the chunk does not support it.
- At most 1 hint per array. If support is weak, use an empty array.
- Keep "quote" under 120 characters and "reason" under 80 characters.
- Keep the whole JSON compact; this memo is only a guide, not a report.
- "document_summary" must stay grounded in the source.
- The memo is only a guide for downstream extraction, not final candidates.
